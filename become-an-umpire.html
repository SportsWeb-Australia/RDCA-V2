Become an Umpire

Join the RDCA Umpires' Association — for ex-players and newcomers alike.

Umpires wanted — we want you! New members are always welcome on the RDCA umpiring panel.
Join the RDCA Umpires' Association

The RDCA Umpires' Association is always on the lookout for new members — particularly those who have played the game but have, for whatever reason, decided to stop playing. In recent years the panel has been well supported by ex-players who have stayed involved by joining the umpiring fraternity.

Ongoing training programs build on the experience you already have as a player, making the move into umpiring much easier than it is for those with little background in the game. The Association has also had real success welcoming non-playing enthusiasts — both men and women — and guiding them through training and accreditation courses to build their knowledge of the Laws.

Clubs have an important part to play. Club executives are encouraged to canvass their members and supporters to find anyone no longer playing who would like to stay part of the game as an RDCA umpire.

Umpire payments are set by the Association each season.
