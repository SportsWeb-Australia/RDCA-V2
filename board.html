About the Association

The Ringwood & District Cricket Association was formed in season 1920/21 and has fostered grassroots cricket and community sport in Melbourne's outer eastern suburbs ever since.

In August 2020 the RDCA amalgamated with the Yarra Valley Cricket Association (YVCA) — founded in 1906 and serving the Yarra Valley for 114 years — to continue under the RDCA name. The Association is a member of the Victorian Metropolitan Cricket Union (VMCU), which in turn reports to Cricket Victoria.

Its member clubs cater for male and female players of all ages across Junior, Senior, Veterans and Women's competitions, each administered by its own committee that reports to the Board of Management.
