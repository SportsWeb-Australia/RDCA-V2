Community Notices

Important association‑wide announcements for clubs, players, officials and volunteers. New notices are sent to subscribers as push notifications.

Get notified instantly. Tap Turn on alerts and your browser will ask for permission to send notifications. Once you allow it, every new RDCA community notice — ground closures, washouts, AGM and event reminders — pops up on your phone or computer, even when this site is closed. Tap a notification to jump straight to the full detail here. You can turn alerts off anytime in your browser or device settings, and we never use them for spam.
Association
AGM scheduled 28 January — all member clubs invited

The RDCA Annual General Meeting is scheduled for 28 January. All member clubs are invited and are encouraged to send at least one delegate.

Formal notice, the agenda and annual reports are circulated to club secretaries beforehand. Any items clubs wish to raise should be submitted to the Board in advance of the meeting.

Grounds
Ground closures in effect — check conditions before travel

Following recent weather, a number of grounds are closed or under assessment. Players and officials should check the latest ground status before travelling, as conditions can change at short notice on match mornings.

Club ground managers will update availability as council assessments are completed. When in doubt, confirm with your club before heading out.

RDCA Awards Night — nominations open until 31 January

Nominations for the RDCA Annual Awards Night are open until 31 January. Clubs can nominate players, volunteers and teams across all grades — senior, junior, women's and veterans.
