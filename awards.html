Awards

The individual and club awards presented across every RDCA grade each season.

RDCA season awards are presented at the Annual Awards Night, celebrating the best players, clubs and volunteers across senior, junior, women's and veterans cricket. Live batting and bowling averages are published throughout the season on PlayHQ.
Award Categories
Recognised across each grade and division every season.

All-Rounder Trophy

Club Championship
Best-performed club across all grades
Best & Fairest
Umpire-voted player of the season by grade
Service & Volunteer
Recognising outstanding contribution off the field
