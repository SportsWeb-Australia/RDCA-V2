Communications

How the RDCA keeps clubs, players, parents, officials and volunteers informed — the channels we use, what each one is for, and what we recommend you switch on so nothing important gets missed.

How we reach you
Different messages travel on different channels. Urgent, time-critical updates go by SMS and push; everything else by email, the website and social. Here's what each channel carries.
Recommended
Email
As needed · low volume
Official association and club communication — registration, season information, important notices, newsletters and event invitations.
Best for: things you'll want a record of.
Opt in: register with your club — your email joins the club & RDCA lists.
Recommended
SMS / Text
Rare · urgent only
Time-critical alerts only — ground closures and washouts, last-minute fixture or venue changes, and team-selection messages from your club.
Best for: "you need to know this today" moments.
Opt in: keep a current mobile number with your club / team app.
Recommended
Push Notifications
Real-time · opt-in
Instant alerts from this website / app — new community notices, results and breaking news, delivered to your phone or computer even when the site is closed.
Best for: the fastest heads-up, free and association-run.
Opt in: turn on alerts on the Community Notices page.
Community Notices
Ongoing · on-site
Association-wide announcements in one place — AGM and meeting notices, policy updates, ground-status notices and event reminders. Push-enabled so new notices reach you instantly.
Best for: the official source of record.
Find it: Community Notices — no sign-up to read.
Social Media
Regular · public
Community updates, match-day photos, results highlights, celebrations and the wider Victorian and Australian cricket conversation — on Facebook and Instagram.
Best for: the fun, the photos and the community feel.
Opt in: follow the RDCA social channels.
Website & Match Centre
Always on
Fixtures, results, ladders, live scores, news and club information — available any time, no sign-up. Add the site to your home screen for a one-tap app experience.
Best for: checking scores, draws and ladders on the go.
Find it: Live Match Centre & Competition Hub.
