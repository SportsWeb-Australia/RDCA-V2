About the Community Big Bash
A relaxed, high-energy Twenty20 format designed to bring the RDCA community together.

The Community Big Bash is the RDCA's short-format Twenty20 competition — built around fast, entertaining cricket that's easy to come and watch and even easier to play. Matches are wrapped up in a single session, making it perfect for players who want competitive cricket without a full two-day commitment.

It's designed to be welcoming: club regulars, returning players, newcomers and social cricketers all have a place. The focus is on participation, atmosphere and getting families and supporters along to the ground for a great night or afternoon of cricket.

Fixtures, results, ladders and registration for the Big Bash are managed on the dedicated RDCA T20 site — use the link below to head straight there.
