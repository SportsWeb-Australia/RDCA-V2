Child Safety

The RDCA is committed to the safety and wellbeing of all children and young people involved in our competitions.

RDCA has zero tolerance for child abuse. We are committed to providing a safe, welcoming and inclusive environment for every child and young person, and we expect all clubs, officials, coaches, volunteers and members to share that commitment.
